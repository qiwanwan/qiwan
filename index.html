修改root密码 ALTER USER 'root'@'localhost' IDENTIFIED BY 'new_password'; flush privileges; 允许root用户远端访问数据库 use mysql; GRANT ALL PRIVILEGES ON . TO 'root'@'%' IDENTIFIED BY 'new_password'; // new_password为你设置的root访问密码 flush privileges; 使用以下命令可查看是否修改成功 select user,authentication_string,Host from user;
